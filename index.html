Cross Cultural Awareness

What is Cross Cultural Awareness? Why is it important?

Cultural awareness is a way of thinking about and viewing the world. It means understanding, respecting and
successfully interacting with those whose values, behaviours, customs and practices are different than your own.
When you achieve Cross Cultural Awareness, you are able to show sensitivity and acceptance of the customs, practices
and behaviour of people from different sociocultural and religious backgrounds within Singapore.
It is especially important to achieve Cross Cultural Awareness as Singapore is made up of diverse cultures and religions.
Respecting the culture of different communities in Singapore is essential to maintaining peace and harmony.

How to develop Cross Cultural Awareness?

Identify and recognise the complexity and richness of the other cultures and religions in Singapore. See the potential benefits of their values and beliefs. Find opportunities to learn more about other cultures and through repeated exposure to develop
deeper understanding of a culture’s unique traits, values and norms.
