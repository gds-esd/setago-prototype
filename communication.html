What is Communication

Effective Communication is an important skill to develop as it is important relay
clear and coherent information to different audiences, purposes and contexts/ situations.
It includes interacting with people from diverse backgrounds through different platforms and being open to different perspectives.
If you are an effective communicator, you are able to construct knowledge, and new understandings and ideas.
