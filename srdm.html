Sound Reasoning and Decision-Making

What is Sound Reasoning and Decision-Making?

Sound reasoning and Decision-Making is the ability to develop well-constructed explanations and well-substantiated conclusions by seeking
understanding through questioning, analysis, comparison,inference/interpretation, evaluation, and synthesis of evidence and arguments.
In order to do so, you need to use evidence and adopt different viewpoints to explain your reasoning and decisions.
It also help if you are able to reflect on your thoughts, attitudes, behaviour and actions.

How to develop Sound Reasoning and Decision-Making skills?

One way to develop this skill is to understand the different elements involved in making a good decision.
